Our Obsession

We hate shopping for clothes and we're obsessed with fixing that. Too often we find ourselves drifting from store to store, searching racks of pre-sized clothes hoping we can find something - anything - that will fit us and our wardrobe. Technology is changing the way we interact with our world and buying clothes is no exception.

We're obsessed with building the next innovation in buying clothes. We believe in a world where buying clothes is easier than ordering a coffee.

Made to fit you

We'll ask your measurements during the purchase process and make your shirt specifically for you.

Don't have a friend or a measuring tape handy? Our fancy AI algorithm can work with just your height and weight.

Durable

Our shirts are designed to last years. We use top-quality cotton that is picked specifically for its strength as well as feel. We have our sewers use short stitches which are stronger. If your shirt wears out, let us know and we promise to make it right.

Looks great

We partnered with an expert tailor to develop the shirt and, together, we obsessed over every detail from pleats to placket. This shirt looks great with or without a jacket. Want to really impress? Just tuck it in.

Affordable

We drive the price down by automating as much as we can without sacrificing quality and focusing on repeat business from impressed customers like you. Not happy with the shirt you receive? We offer a full refund.

We believe in a world where buying clothes is easier than ordering a coffee.
